1️⃣ GDP per capita — Distribution

Mean: $20,346 | Median: $7,588 | 10th–90th pct: $1,000 – $53,383

Interpretation.
As shown in Figure Figure 1, the distribution of GDP per capita in 2022 is highly right-skewed, with a mean of $20,346 and a median of $7,588. This large gap indicates that a few very high-income countries pull up the global average. Most countries lie between $1,000 and $53,000 (10th–90th percentile), showing that the majority of economies fall in low- to middle-income ranges.
